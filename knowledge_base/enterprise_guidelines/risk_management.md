# Risk Management Guidelines

## Purpose

Every project should identify potential risks early and define mitigation strategies before implementation.

## Common Risk Categories

- Technical Risk
- Schedule Risk
- Security Risk
- Budget Risk
- Operational Risk

## Best Practices

- Identify risks during planning.
- Estimate impact and likelihood.
- Assign an owner for each risk.
- Define mitigation strategies.
- Review risks regularly throughout the project.

## Example

Risk

External API becomes unavailable.

Mitigation

- Retry failed requests.
- Use exponential backoff.
- Fallback to cached data.
- Log failures for investigation.

## Common Mistakes

- Ignoring low-probability risks.
- No mitigation strategy.
- No ownership.
- Risks documented but never reviewed.

## Summary

Effective risk management improves project reliability and helps teams respond quickly to unexpected issues.
